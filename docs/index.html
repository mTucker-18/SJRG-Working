Friends of the San Juaquin River Gorge

The SJRG is in the heart of wildland-urban interface

Friends of the San Joaquin River Gorge is a not-for-profit 501(c)(​3) established in 2015 to assist the BLM. Fr​​iends Of the San Joaquin River Gorge (​Fr.​O.G​​) seek to ignite, inspire and cultivate visitor’s connection with our local nature, culture and history. Our organization is designed to engage, and foster stewardship and promote ethical preservation of natural, cultural and historic resources. We aim to connect partners, patrons, visitors and volunteers to our communities via evocative educational, interactive and hands-on experiences.
