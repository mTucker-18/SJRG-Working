Upcoming Events

Perseid Meteor Shower

When: July 17 - August 26 (peak: Aug 12-13th)

The Perseids (pur-see-ids) are a popular meteor shower in the northern hemisphere because they peak on warm summer nights. At it's maximum you might see 50-75 meteors per hour. This particular shower will also take place during an nearly full moon, so bring your teliscope!

For more information check out the American Meteor Society's website here.

Nature Hikes

The San Joaquin River Gorge is home to hundreds of plant and animal species. Experience them first-hand on a nature hike!

What animals might I find? Bald eagles, golden eagles, Swainson's hawks, mule deer, bob cats, mountain lions, frogs, salamanders, lizards, skinks, turtles, chipmonks, ducks, geese, foxes, and many, many more.

For more information check out the San Joaquin River Partnership's website here.
