Plan Your Trip

Welcome to the San Joaquin River Gorge Recreation Area, the best kept secret in this area! Come explore the last scenic and wild portion of the San Joaquin River. Connect with nature through an abundance of outdoor opportunities for families, friends, scouts, your group and more. Getting here is easy and accessible year-round!

Getting here is easy and accessible by paved roads year-round! The San Joaquin River Gorge is rich in outdoor fun activities, significant natural, cultural and historic resources and is a rare Sierra Nevada transition zone, very important for the vigor of nature, health of wildlife and refuge/retreat for people.
