Perseid Meteor Shower

When? July 17 - August 26 (peak: Aug 12-13th)

The Perseids (pur-see-ids) are a popular meteor shower in the northern hemisphere because they peak on warm summer nights. At it's maximum you might see 50-75 meteors per hour. This particular shower will also take place during an almost full moon, so bring your teliscope!

For more information check out the American Meteor Society's website here .
